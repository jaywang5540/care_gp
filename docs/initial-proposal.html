🏥 Medicare GP Assistant

AI-Powered Medicare Benefits Schedule Billing System

NextGen25 Challenge 8 - Initial Proposal

Executive Summary

We are developing Medicare GP Assistant, an AI-powered system that transforms GP consultations into Medicare-compliant billing in under 30 seconds. Our solution addresses the critical pain point where Australian GPs spend 30% of their time on administrative tasks instead of patient care.

🚀 Key Innovation: Natural language processing of consultation notes → Automatic MBS code recommendation → Compliant billing generation

🎯 Demonstration Scenarios

Scenario 1: Standard Consultation

Input: "Patient presents with headache for 3 days, tension-type, prescribed paracetamol"
Duration: 15 minutes
→ Recommends: Item 23 (Level B consultation)
→ Confidence: High
→ Compliance: ✓ All checks passed

Scenario 2: Mental Health Assessment

Input: "Depression screening, GAD-7 score 12, referred to psychologist"
Duration: 25 minutes
→ Recommends: Item 2713 (Mental Health Treatment Plan)
→ Generates: Referral letter, Care plan
→ Confidence: High

Scenario 3: Chronic Disease Management

Input: "Diabetes review, HbA1c 8.2, medication adjustment"
Duration: 20 minutes
→ Recommends: Item 732 (GP Management Plan Review)
→ Compliance: ✓ Last plan >3 months ago
→ Documents: Updated care plan
